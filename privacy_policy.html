Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 11, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how 1 Minute Sales collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information. This policy applies to all users of our platform, including individual contributors, sales managers, and organizational administrators.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Name, email address, phone number, company name, and job title.
  • Account preferences, user roles, and team structure.
  • Billing and payment information for subscription management.

Business & Contact Data:

  • Customer and prospect information uploaded or imported by you or your organization.
  • Call logs, email communications, and engagement history (when integrated with your CRM).
  • Lead scoring data, conversation transcripts, and performance metrics.

Usage & Analytics Data:

  • Pages visited, features used, time spent in the platform, and interaction patterns.
  • Device information (browser type, operating system, IP address).
  • Performance data and system logs for debugging and optimization.

Communications:

  • Support tickets, feedback, and messages sent to our team.
  • Email communications regarding account updates, features, and service announcements.

2. How We Use Information

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our platform, including AI-powered features, call recording, and reporting capabilities.
  • Account Management: To manage subscriptions, process payments, handle access requests, and support multi-user team environments.
  • Communication: To send service updates, feature announcements, billing notices, and responses to your inquiries.
  • Analytics & Optimization: To analyze usage patterns, improve user experience, identify bugs, and optimize platform performance.
  • AI Model Training: To improve our AI-powered sales scripts, response recommendations, and scheduling features (unless you opt out).
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with laws, regulations (including GDPR, CCPA, TCPA), respond to legal requests, and prevent fraud or misuse.
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: To detect, prevent, and investigate unauthorized access, security threats, or policy violations.

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

Third-Party Service Providers: We share information with vendors who assist in operating the platform, including hosting providers, payment processors, and analytics services. These partners are bound by confidentiality agreements.

CRM Integrations: When you authorize integrations with HubSpot, Pipedrive, or Zoho, we share relevant data according to the scope of permissions you grant. This data is governed by the third party's privacy policy.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, government request, or court order. We will attempt to notify you of such disclosures when permitted.

Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any change in ownership or control.

No Sale of Data: We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Data Security & Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.
  • Role-based access controls for multi-user teams.
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing.
  • Secure credential management and two-factor authentication support.
  • Regular backups and disaster recovery procedures.

While we implement robust security measures, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

5. Your Data Rights & Choices

Access & Portability: You have the right to request a copy of your personal information in a portable format.

Correction & Deletion: You may update or request deletion of your personal information. For business data within your account, you maintain control and can delete or export it at any time.

Opt-Out of Marketing: You can opt out of promotional emails and marketing communications through the unsubscribe link in our emails or by contacting us.

AI Training Opt-Out: You may opt out of having your data used for AI model improvement by contacting our support team.

Cookie & Tracking: Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent.

GDPR & CCPA Rights: If you are a resident of the EU or California, you have additional rights under GDPR and CCPA, including the right to know, delete, and object to processing. Contact us to exercise these rights.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After account termination, we retain data for:

  • Financial records: typically 7 years for tax and compliance purposes.
  • Usage analytics: aggregated and anonymized data retained indefinitely.
  • Personal information: deleted or anonymized within 90 days of account closure, unless legal obligations require longer retention.

7. Third-Party Links & Services

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites and services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover a child has provided information, we will delete it promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States or other countries. By using our Service, you consent to such transfers. We implement appropriate safeguards, including Standard Contractual Clauses for GDPR compliance.

10. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, remember your preferences, and analyze usage patterns. These include:

  • Session Cookies: Essential for platform functionality and security.
  • Persistent Cookies: Remember your login status and preferences.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of material changes via email or platform notification. Your continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of updated terms.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected, used, and shared.
  • Delete personal information collected from you.
  • Opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information.
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

To submit a CCPA request, contact us at privacy@1minutesales.com.

13. GDPR Compliance (EU Residents)

For users subject to GDPR, we process your data based on:

  • Contract Performance: Processing necessary to provide our services.
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
  • Legitimate Interest: Improving security, analytics, and platform optimization.
  • Consent: For marketing communications and optional data usage.

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Our Data Protection Officer can be reached at privacy@1minutesales.com.

14. TCPA Compliance

We comply with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) by ensuring that contact lists are verified, consent is obtained where required, and do-not-call registries are respected. Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with TCPA regulations when using our calling and messaging features.

15. Contact & Data Protection Officer

For privacy-related questions, concerns, or to exercise your rights, contact:

  • Email: privacy@1minutesales.com
  • General Support: info@1minutesales.com

We typically respond to data requests within 30 days. For comprehensive information about our privacy practices or custom data processing agreements, please contact our team.
