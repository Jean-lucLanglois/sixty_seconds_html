# 1 Minute Sales Website

Static website for 1 Minute Sales. This repo contains the public marketing pages, contact form, and legal documents.

## Pages

1. `index.html` – landing page
2. `contact.html` – contact form and direct contact info
3. `register.html` – registration page
4. `careers.html` – careers page
5. `privacy_policy.html` – privacy policy
6. `terms_of_service.html` – terms of service

## Assets

1. `img/` – site images and logos
2. `blog/` – blog index and posts
3. `docs/` – supporting documents
4. `1-minute-sales*.svg/png` – brand assets

## Local Preview

1. Open `index.html` directly in a browser.
2. Or run a simple static server:

```bash
python3 -m http.server 8080
```

Then visit `http://localhost:8080`.

## Notes

1. The contact form posts to a webhook configured in `contact.html`.
2. A2P SMS consent disclosures are in `contact.html` and `privacy_policy.html`.
