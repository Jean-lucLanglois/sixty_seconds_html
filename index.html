Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to what comes up most on strategy calls.

What does 1 Minute Sales actually do?+−

We act as your outsourced sales force. When a lead comes in, our team responds within 60 seconds, qualifies them, books appointments, and closes on your behalf. You receive booked appointments and signed contracts — not a tool to operate. Prefer to run it in-house? Use the same platform yourself.

Can my own team use the platform?+−

Yes. Become a member and your agents work every lead from one business number — calls, texts, and email auto-sync to the right lead. Full transcripts, AI Sales Advisor, call logging, KPIs, and manager tools included, so no communication is lost across reps or devices.

How quickly will leads be responded to?+−

Within 60 seconds, 24/7, across phone, SMS, and email — whether we work the leads or your team does on our platform.

What is your pricing model?+−

Done-for-you engagements are a monthly retainer plus commission on closed deals, scoped to your business. Platform membership is a subscription. We tailor both on a strategy call to your lead volume and deal size.

Do I have to change my CRM or tools?+−

No. We plug into the CRM, calendar, phone, and lead sources you already use — HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, Google, Outlook and more. No migration, no IT project.
