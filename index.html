What is 1 Minute Sales?

1 Minute Sales is a US-based outsourced sales service that handles inbound lead response, qualification, appointment booking, and deal closing for high-ticket businesses that cannot afford to lose leads to slow follow-up. The company specializes in industries where each inbound inquiry carries significant revenue — personal injury law firms, cosmetic surgery practices, med spas, dental implant groups, custom home builders, remodelers, and premium roofing companies. 1 Minute Sales responds to inbound leads within 60 seconds, 24 hours a day, across phone, SMS, and email, then qualifies the contact, books a consultation or appointment directly to the client's calendar, and where applicable closes the deal and sends contracts. The service operates on a monthly retainer plus commission model and integrates with existing CRM platforms (HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho) without requiring clients to change their current tools.

What we do for you

We run the entire sales motion on your behalf. You hand us leads — we hand you booked appointments, signed contracts, and weekly reports.

60-Second Lead Response

When a lead hits your inbox or form, our team is on it in under 60 seconds — 24/7. Contacting a lead inside the first minute makes them 21× more likely to qualify than waiting 30 minutes (InsideSales / Oldroyd et al., "The Short Life of Online Sales Leads," Harvard Business Review, 2011).

Multi-Channel Outreach

Phone, SMS, and email. We work every lead across every channel until we reach them — not just one weak attempt that gets ignored.

Appointment Booking

We book qualified consults and meetings directly into your calendar — only the leads worth your time, properly briefed before they show up.

Qualifying & Nurturing

We filter tire-kickers, follow up on the warm ones for as long as it takes, and only pass through prospects who are ready to buy.

Closing & Contracts

We don't just hand off warm leads — we close. Contracts sent, signed, and (where you allow) financed. You execute the work; we deliver the deal.

Transparent Reporting

Weekly reports show every lead worked, response times, appointments booked, and deals closed. You stay in control without being in the weeds.
